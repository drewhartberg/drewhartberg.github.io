My Story

It was very clear from an early age that I was extremely curious with how things worked. This initially manifested in pointlessly tearing apart forgotten toys to stare at the inner workings. After serveral years I stopped pulling apart the physical layers of toys and started to dive deep into the actual processes of various topics. Cooking, Music, Filmmaking, and most recently, Software Engineering.
