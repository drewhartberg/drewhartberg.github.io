Drew Hartberg - 25 - Madison, WI

My Story

It was clear from an early age that I was extremely curious with how things worked. This initially manifested in pointlessly tearing apart forgotten toys to stare at the inner workings. After several years I stopped pulling apart the physical layers of toys and started to dive deep into the actual processes of various topics. Cooking, Music, Filmmaking, and now I've settled into making a living in Web Development.

Moving several times while growing up played a huge role in making me who I am today. On the surface, moving gave me the ability to draw upon the unique strengths of each community. But digging into the core, I've noticed that it taught me resilience and the importance of being close with those around you. No matter what life threw at me, even uprooting and flipping my world upside down, I always came out okay, if not better, on the other side. That was undoubtedly because of my family around me. That's naturally morphed into my mentality in the workplace and with my coworkers. We may go through hell, but if we're together, we'll make it out okay on the other side.
